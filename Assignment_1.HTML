APJ Abdul Kalam

Biography

APJ Abdul Kalam, born , 1931, was a renowned Indian scientist and politician who served as the-11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Known as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology, Kalam was deeply admired for his humility, educational advocacy, and dedication to public service. His legacy includes inspiring millions through his visionary ideas and compassionate approach to leadership.

-Wikipedia
